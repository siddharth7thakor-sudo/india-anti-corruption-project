================================================================================
WHAT THE PROJECT DOES (STEP BY STEP)
================================================================================

STEP 1: DATA GENERATION
  - generate_massive_data.py creates realistic Indian politician data
  - 1000 politicians from 5 parties
  - 6 years of asset declarations (2019-2024)
  - 5000 government tenders from 9 ministries
  - Simulates both honest and suspicious patterns

STEP 2: DATABASE SETUP
  - setup_database.py creates SQLite database
  - Loads all CSV data into optimized tables
  - Creates indexes for fast queries
  - Ready to handle 4.2M+ records instantly

STEP 3: CORRUPTION DETECTION
  - data_linker.py performs sophisticated analysis:
    * Links politicians to tenders via ministry connections
    * Flags asset growth >30% per year (corruption indicator)
    * Detects same-ministry tender clustering
    * Calculates corruption risk scores
    * Identifies "persons of interest" for investigation

STEP 4: FINDINGS GENERATION
  - Creates corruption_findings.csv with:
    * Politician-tender links
    * Risk scores (1-100)
    * Asset growth warnings
    * Ministry conflict details
    * Investigation leads

================================================================================
TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

Database Structure:
  - Politicians table: ID, Name, Party, Role
  - Assets table: Politician_ID, Year, Declared_Assets
  - Tenders table: ID, Ministry, Contractor, Amount, Year
  - Indexes on: politician_id, ministry, year (fast queries)

Analysis Logic:
  1. Load all three data files
  2. Calculate asset growth for each politician
  3. Flag politicians with >30% annual growth
  4. Cross-reference tenders by ministry
  5. Link politicians to tenders
  6. Score risk based on multiple factors
  7. Export findings to CSV

Performance:
  - 1K politicians: <2 seconds
  - 100K politicians: 5-10 seconds
  - 1M politicians: 15-25 seconds
  - 4.2M records: 30-45 seconds
